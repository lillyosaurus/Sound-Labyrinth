An accessible videogame

Sound Labyrinth is an accessible videogame which is designed to provide the same experience to people with a range of sensory abilities. People with low vision or blindness can navigate the game by audio input, while people who have hearing impairments can navigate via visual input. The game aims to provide the same experience across all media forms.
